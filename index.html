About Us

Located in the heart of Legian, Fresco Bali is serving up the freshest seasonal fare with a focus on locally-sourced produce and markets. Our head chef sources only the freshest ingredients that have been grown in Bali using well-established organic methods for an array of healthy and delectable fresh dishes. We're open daily for breakfast, lunch, dinner from 8am to 10:00pm and reservations are recommended as we do get busy.
